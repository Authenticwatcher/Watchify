Tag Heuer Carrera Calibre 16
The Tag Heuer Carrera Calibre 16 (CV2011) features a 41mm steel case paired with a black aluminum tachymeter bezel. Its silver dial is adorned with metallic hands and hour markers with luminous components for enhanced low-light legibility, along with a date window at 3 o’clock and three chronograph subdials at 6, 9, and 12 o’clock. Completing its design is a sturdy steel bracelet secured by a fold clasp.
