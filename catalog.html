Tag Heuer Carrera Calibre 16

The Tag Heuer Carrera Calibre 16 (CV2011) features a 41mm steel case paired with a black aluminum tachymeter bezel. Its silver dial is adorned with metallic hands and hour markers with luminous components for enhanced low-light legibility, along with a date window at 3 o’clock and three chronograph subdials at 6, 9, and 12 o’clock. Completing its design is a sturdy steel bracelet secured by a fold clasp.

Tag Heuer Carrera Calibre 16 Chrono

High functionality and masculine styling make this Calibre 16 Automatic Chronograph a necessity for all lovers of motor racing. A stylish day and date function is positioned at 3 o'clock. A high-tech Calibre 16 automatic movement powers the chronograph functions like central seconds, plus minutes and hours. An open flange design with red accents and large numerals allow for easy readability. The sapphire crystal fitted into the caseback reveals the movement in action.
